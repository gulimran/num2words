Take a number and give the equivalent number in british english words e.g.
	1 = one
	21 = twenty one
	105 = one hundred and five
	56945781 = fifty six million nine hundred and forty five thousand seven hundred and eighty one etc.. up to 999,999,999

USAGE: java -jar num2word.jar <number>, where number is between 1 and 999999999
